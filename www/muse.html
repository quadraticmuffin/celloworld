MUSE Project

The Music Unites: Songs for Equity (MUSE) project is an initiative by Cello World and the MIT Ribotones to highlight the works of Black composers. Over the 2020-21 academic year, we will be releasing performances of works by Black composers online. Each performance will benefit a charity supporting music education for underrepresented minorities in music, which viewers are encouraged to donate to.

If you have any questions about the MUSE project, or if you would like to participate, please contact us at muse-project-exec@mit.edu. All instruments are welcome in the MUSE project.
